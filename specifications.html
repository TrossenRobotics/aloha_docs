ALOHA Pre-configured Laptop

Trossen Robotics offers the option of providing a laptop pre-configured to work with either variant of the ALOHA kit. The laptop is the Serval WS (serw13) from System76 with the following specifications:

  • Ubuntu 22.04 LTS
  • 5.8 GHz i9-14900HX
  • 32 GB DDR5 4800 MHz (2x 16GB)
  • 1 TB PCIe4 M.2 SSD
  • WiFi 6E + Bluetooth 5
  • 8 GB GeForce RTX 4060 with 3072 CUDA Cores
  • 17.3” Matte 2K QHD (240Hz) Display
